Instructor Notes
Slide 1: Introduction to mIRC
Emphasize the importance of proper mIRC configuration for secure military communications. Highlight the key features that make mIRC suitable for tactical operations.
Slide 2: Installation Requirements
Ensure students understand system requirements and security protocols. Mention the importance of using approved versions only in operational environments.
Slide 3: Network Configuration
Walk through each network setting carefully. Students often struggle with proxy configurations - be prepared to provide additional examples.
Slide 4: Security Settings
Critical section - emphasize OPSEC considerations. Discuss the importance of encryption settings and secure channel management.
Common Issues & Solutions
Be prepared to address connection timeouts, authentication failures, and firewall conflicts. Have backup slides ready for troubleshooting scenarios.
